New product SKU or product relabeling
SSBS request process — 13 May 2026
Reference guide
TIR operations
Note: The processes described in this document will be updated later this year as TELUS introduces program improvements related to Web Console. New "status" fields will be added and the process below will change.
1 Process steps
1
Collect product information
1a Gather 100 good quality photos where the new product or newly relabeled product is visible on the shelf.
1b Collect the product catalog pack shot.
1c Collect required product information (example: product number, category, dimensions, etc.).
Owner: Customer
2
Create ticket in Salesforce (or ServiceNow for Nestlé)
Upon completing the Product Catalog Template, the customer should create a request ticket in Salesforce. For Nestlé, they should create the ticket within ServiceNow, and it will flow to Salesforce.
Owner: Customer
3
Complete the Product Catalog Template
Fill out the Product Catalog Template with all required product details. The product catalog photo must also be inserted into the template. This is the same template used earlier in the project.
Owner: TELUS
4
Commercial agreements (SOW)
An SOW should be created, delivered and approved between TELUS and the customer regarding the approved scope and cost of the requested changes.
Owner: TELUS
5
Create request ticket in SSBS system
Market/Customer submits a request ticket in SSBS's ticketing system with the completed template and photos attached.
Owner: TELUS
6
Product training
SoftServe will conduct product training and deliver the results into the UAT environment if available or directly into production. If there are issues, work with SoftServe to resolve them.
Owner: SoftServe
Closing procedures
7
Close ticket and invoice
After successful implementation of the product retraining, TELUS should close the ticket within Salesforce and ServiceNow and proceed to invoice the SOW.
Owner: TELUS
2 Photo requirements
100 good quality on-shelf photos where the new or relabeled product is clearly visible. These photos are used by SSBS for product recognition training.
3 Important notes
Market/Customer responsibility — master data & configuration
The Market/Customer is responsible for all required master data setup and configuration of the product. This is the same setup performed during the original project: scene type setup, product hierarchies, competitor brand flag, monitor setup, etc.
Minimum training request
Minimum number of products for one requested training is 10. If there are fewer than 10 products, the request will be charged for 10.
Retraining cost
The Nestlé agreed cost for retraining is $18 per SKU. Other customers may vary.
